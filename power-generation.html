Engineering & Support

Project & Solutions

Beyond supplying machinery, M.S.Trade International provides custom-engineered turnkey power setups. We design, construct, and install automatic synchronizing panels, automatic mains failure (AMF) controls, custom fuel lines, soundproof acoustic treatments, and structural foundations.

  • AMF & Auto-Transfer System: Seamless transition back and forth from utility mains grid in milliseconds.
  • Multi-Genset Synchronization: Synchronize multiple units to match load distribution demands dynamically.
  • Soundproofing Acoustics: Structural acoustics custom-tailored to site layouts, ensuring noise compliance.
  • Fuel Management Solutions: Integrated bulk storage tank setups and automated fuel feed systems.
